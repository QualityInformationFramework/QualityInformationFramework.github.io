Element StatisticalStudyResults
Annotations
 
The global StatisticalStudyResults element is the head of a
substitution group. Any element that is a member of the group may
be substituted for the StatisticalStudyResults element anywhere the
StatisticalStudyResults element may be used in an instance file.
The members of this substitution group are:
  BiasStudyResults
  CapabilityStudyResults
  FirstArticleStudyResults
  GageRandRStudyResults
  LinearityStudyResults
  SimpleStudyResults
  StabilityStudyResults
  ProcessDifferenceStudyResults
  ProductionStudyResults
