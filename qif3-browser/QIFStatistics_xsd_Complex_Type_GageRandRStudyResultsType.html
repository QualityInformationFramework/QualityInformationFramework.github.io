Complex Type GageRandRStudyResultsType
Annotations
 
The GageRandRStudyResultsType defines information that is related to
the results of a gage repeatability and reproducibility statistical
study where a number of part samples were repeatedly measured by
two or more appraisers to establish the repeatability and
reproducibility of a measurement process.
