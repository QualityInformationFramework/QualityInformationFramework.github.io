Element OppositeAngledLinesFromScanType / SearchRadius
Annotations
 
The SearchRadius element is the radius around the nominal feature, wherein the measured feature can be expected. The SearchRadius is the radius added to and subtracted from the size of the nominal feature defining an extruded shell. All scanned points within this extruded shell are used for the retrieval of the feature. The extruded shell's axis is defined by the feature's normal and the extruded shell's axis passes through the feature's center point. The extruded shell is evenly disposed about the nominal opposite angled lines feature.
